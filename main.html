A Recent Graduate

... from the University of Surrey with a BSc(Hons) degree in Computing and Information technology where I learnt primarily computer science topics supplemented with business management and entrepreneurship modules. I was honored to be awarded the "IBM Prize for Best Final Year Performance".

As a part of my studies, I completed an industrial placement year at Microsoft as a software engineer working on Paint 3D in London. This led to the opportunity to work on the Xbox team at Microsoft's HQ in Redmond, WA for a period of three months. I was incredibly fortunate to work on both of these teams who I learnt so much from. For my contributions towards my workplaces, my peers, and my university for work completed during my placement, I was awarded the "Best Placement Student of the Year for the Faculty of Engineering and Physical Science" and the "IBM Prize for Excellent Placement Year Performance".
