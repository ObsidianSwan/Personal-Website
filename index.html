A University of Surrey Graduate

I earned a first-class BSc(Hons) degree in Computing and Information technology where I learnt primarily computer science topics supplemented with business management, finance, and entrepreneurship modules. I was honored to be awarded the "IBM Prize for Best Final Year Performance".

As a part of my studies, I completed an industrial placement year at Microsoft as a software engineer working on Paint 3D in London. This led to the opportunity to work on the Xbox team at Microsoft's HQ in Redmond, WA for a period of three months. I was incredibly fortunate to work on both of these teams from whom I learnt so much.

@HerHelloWorld

I founded HerHelloWorld in 2018 to mentor new technologists, create an inclusive tech-centered community, and share the dynamic nature of the tech industry in the aim of helping build a more representative industry. HerHelloWorld is where I answer early-in-career questions, review books, discuss changes in technology, share events that I attend or speak at, and address barriers for women entering STEM.
